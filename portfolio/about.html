About me

Get to know me!

Musician, artist, web designer

Hi, I'm Olivia! I'm a sophomore at Eastside Preparatory School. I like to sing classical music, draw and paint, play piano, and make websites.


Classical singer

I'm a classical mezzo-soprano and I've been singing classical music for 5 years. I've won state and regional competitions and competeted at the national level. Recently, I won gold in the 2022 National Associations of Teachers of Singing (NATS) state awards and placed second in the 2021 NATS regional competition. This year is my second year in Seattle Opera's advanced youth program, Teen Vocal Studio. There, I worked with renowned professionals such as Emily Fons, Brandie Sutton, and Stacey Mastrian.

Classical pianist

I've been playing piano for 10 years, winning multiple competitions. Recently, I won silver in the Russian Chamber Music Ensemble and Washington Music Teachers National Association Competition's piano duet divisions with my sister. I've also performed live on the local classical music radio station 98.1 KING FM's Northwest Focus program.


Artist

I like drawing and painting. I don't have a lot of awards or cool events I did to prove my skill, but please check out my art page to see some of my art. I drew the cover art for Oliver Crosby's single "Kalimoxto", but that's just a quick sketch that I did in OneNote during school. Please check out the song though (it's on pretty much every platform)!


Web design

I made this website myself, so that should show you that I do web design. This is my second year making websites, and I've made some cool websites for class. I can use HTML, CSS, and stumble my way through a JavaScript.
