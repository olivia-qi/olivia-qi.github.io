Classical mezzo-soprano and pianist

Singer

I'm a classical mezzo-soprano and I've been singing classical music for five years. I've won state and regional competitions and competeted at the national level. Recently, I won gold in the 2022 National Associations of Teachers of Singing (NATS) state awards and placed second in the 2021 NATS regional competition. This year is my second year in Seattle Opera's advanced youth program, Teen Vocal Studio. There, I worked with renowned professionals such as Emily Fons, Brandie Sutton, and Stacey Mastrian.
